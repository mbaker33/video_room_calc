Room Kit EQ extended options
Quad Camera Extended (720p) uses the xConfiguration Cameras SpeakerTrack ZoomRange: Extended configuration, which reduces the image from 1080p to 720p but allows for a tighter zoom. Lighting is very important when using this setting.

Quad Cam + PTZ 4K Extended requires the Quad Camera and PTZ 4K camera to extend the SpeakerTrack capability of the Quad Camera. The functionality also requires Table Microphone Pros. See Cross-view and Extended speaker view: setup and guidelines.

Disclaimer:
Camera values are extrapolated from field tests but may not represent the real world results of your room. There is no warranty expressed or implied from the results. Audio is more important than video and can change with room dynamics, so make sure to do proper mic placement.
